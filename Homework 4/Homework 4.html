The Finland Ice Hockey team competed at the 2022 Winter Olympics in Beijing, China, from February 4th to February 20th in 2022. The flagbearer during the opening ceremony for the Finland Ice Hockey team was Valterri Filppula, and the flagbearer during the closing ceremony was the cross-country skier Krista Pärmäkoski. Finland had a haul of two gold medals and eight total medals, which included their first ever gold medal in ice hockey. The ice hockey team that won the gold medal consisted of 25 players and took place on February 20, 2022.
